Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, rich meat sauce (typically made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, and herbs), creamy b√©chamel sauce, and grated cheese, usually mozzarella and Parmesan. The layers are stacked alternately and baked until the pasta is tender and the cheese is melted and golden brown on top. It's a hearty and comforting dish, often served as a main course for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Cook the Lasagna Noodles
  3. Assemble the Lasagna
  4. Top the Lasagna
  5. Bake
